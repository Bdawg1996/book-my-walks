Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need experience to start?
No. That's exactly why Book My Walks exists. We'll walk you through everything and help you build confidence in the brand you're creating — from your first client to a thriving business.
How much can I realistically make?
It depends largely on your area, but full-time dog walkers can earn $3,000–5,000+ a month. We help you understand your local market and set the right rates.
How long until I get my first client?
Many walkers land their first client within the first week. It depends on how fast you move using the playbook we provide.
What do I need to get started?
A genuine love for dogs, a professional mindset, and the drive to build something of your own. We help you handle the rest.
What do I get for $249 a month?
Your own booking page at bookmywalks.com/yourbusinessname, a booking and scheduling system, income tracking tools, weekly group calls, up to one hour one-on-one with Brandon monthly, and email support with replies within 24 hours.
What happens on the monthly one-on-one?
Up to one hour focused entirely on your business. Marketing, getting clients, pricing, business structure, backend setup — whatever you need most that month. No fluff, just answers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime.
What doesn't Book My Walks handle?
Three things — taxes, insurance, and client payments. We don't provide insurance but we help you find the right coverage — most people are surprised, pet business liability typically runs around $200 a year. We give you the tools you need to track your income but we're not accountants. You collect payments directly from clients however works for you — Venmo, Zelle, cash. We never touch your money and never take a cut.
What's the custom domain option?
As your business grows you can upgrade to your own custom domain. Your page moves from bookmywalks.com/brandysdogs to brandysdogs.com — same page, same clients, just your own address on the door. Ask us about it when you're ready.
