Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started

Do I need experience to start?
No. That's exactly why Book My Walks exists. We walk you through everything from your first client to your first $1,000 month.
How much can I realistically make?
Full time dog walkers typically earn $2,500–$3,000 a month. Part time is $800–$1,200. It depends on your city, your rates, and how many clients you take on. We help you figure out the right number on your first call.
How long until I get my first client?
Our goal is your first client within 30 days. Some walkers land one in the first week. It depends on how fast you move — we give you the exact playbook.
What do I need to get started?
A phone, comfortable shoes, and a love for dogs. We handle the rest — your booking page, your business setup, your first client strategy.
Do I need to register a business or get insurance?
It depends on your state. We cover both on your first strategy call. Insurance surprises most people — pet business liability typically runs $300–500 a year, which is less than most people pay for car insurance. We research the best options for your specific city.

What Book My Walks doesn't handle

What doesn't Book My Walks cover?
Three things — taxes, insurance, and client payments. We give you an income tracker spreadsheet that makes tax time simple, but we're not accountants. We don't provide insurance but we help you find affordable coverage on your first call. And you collect payments directly from clients however works for you — Venmo, Zelle, cash, whatever. We never touch your money and we never take a cut.
